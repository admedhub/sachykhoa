Gray’s Atlas of Anatomy 3rd Edition 2021

Richard Drake, A. Wayne Vogl, Adam W. M. Mitchell, Richard Tibbitts, Paul Richardson

2021

Clinically focused, consistently and clearly illustrated, and logically organized, Gray’s Atlas of Anatomy, the companion resource to the popular Gray’s Anatomy for Students, presents a vivid, visual depiction of anatomical structures.
Stunning illustrations demonstrate the correlation of structures with clinical images and surface anatomy – essential for proper identification in the dissection lab and successful preparation for course exams.
